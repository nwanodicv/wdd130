Whitewater Rafting Site Plan

1. Overview

Purpose: To provide comprehensive information about rafting adventures, promote exciting packages, and enable online bookings.

Audience: Adventure seekers, families, and corporate teams looking for thrilling outdoor experiences and team-building activities.

5. Wireframes

Wireframes will show layout for navigation, hero image, tour highlights, and call-to-actions. To be designed during the development phase.

6. Marketing and SEO

Develop a professional and responsive website. Use SEO practices to improve ranking. Engage on platforms like Instagram and Facebook with visual content.

7. Compliance and Safety Information

Include safety guidelines, emergency procedures, required certifications, and a commitment to comply with local regulations to ensure customer trust and confidence.
